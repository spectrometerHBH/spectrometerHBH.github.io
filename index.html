Bohan Hou

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tianqi Chen. I am a member of Catalyst.

My research interests lie in machine learning systems and deep learning compilers.

Prior to coming to CMU, I received my Bachelor's degree in ACM Honors Class of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Weinan Zhang and Prof. Yong Yu.

Projects

MLC-LLM: Machine Learning Compilation for Large Language Models [repo]
MLC-LLM is a universal solution that allows any language models to be deployed natively on a diverse set of hardware backends and native applications, plus a productive framework for everyone to further optimize model performance for their own use cases.
